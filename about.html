Learn About Our Journey

2022We got this

In 2022, Specter celebrated its eighth year in business and its continued success. The firm had established a strong reputation for providing excellent legal services, and had become one of the leading law firms in the country. Despite the challenges it had faced, the firm remained committed to its clients and its values.

20212021

In 2021, Specter faced a class action lawsuit from several former clients who claimed that the firm had overbilled them. After a lengthy investigation, the firm reached a settlement with the clients and agreed to repay a portion of the fees they had charged. The firm also implemented new billing practices to prevent similar incidents in the future.

2020The Pandemic

In 2020, the firm faced the challenges posed by the global pandemic, but was able to adapt and continue to provide legal services to its clients. The firm also continued to grow, hiring several new lawyers and opening a new office in a different city.

2019Fourth year annivesary

The firm celebrated its fourth year in business and its continued success. The firm had grown significantly, with several offices across the country and a team of over 20 lawyers. Specter continued to attract new clients and win high-profile cases.

2018Hit the wall

The firm faced its first major challenge, as one of its partners was accused of unethical behavior. Despite the setback, Specter remained focused on providing high-quality legal services to its clients. The firm also strengthened its internal processes and adopted a strict code of ethics to prevent similar incidents in the future.

2017Fist big case

Specter won its first big case, which brought significant recognition to the firm. The firm's profile was raised, and it became a well-known and respected law firm in the city. The firm also opened a second office in a different city, to better serve its clients.

2016The second year

Specter experienced steady growth and increased its client base. The firm's reputation for providing excellent legal services spread, and they started to attract larger clients. The firm also expanded its team, hiring two new associates to help with the growing workload.

2015The Beginning

The law firm, Specter, was established in 2015 by two young and ambitious lawyers, Simon Mulu and Thuiya Vincent. They started small, with just a few clients and a small office in the downtown area. Despite the challenges, they were determined to build a successful and reputable law firm.
